Soil Analysis

About

Traditional soil analysis methods in precision agriculture present significant challenges, characterized by complexity and a lack of real-time capabilities.

Farmers currently face difficulties in obtaining timely and accurate information about the soil on their farms, hindering their ability to make informed decisions regarding crop selection, irrigation, and fertilization.
This gap in accessibility to real-time soil insights limits the optimization of agricultural practices and, subsequently, impacts crop yields and sustainability. The absence of a user-friendly solution exacerbates the issue, creating an urgent need for an innovative approach to soil analysis that is both accessible and immediate for farmers.

Beaton Ndawa

Electrical and Electronics Engineering
